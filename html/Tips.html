General Tips

  1. Keep in mind that certain Pokemon are shiny locked, meaning they can never be shiny, this generaly applies to legendaries and mythicals
  2. Always research the Pokemon you want to hunt to know if it has moves that forcefuly end the battle, how much PP it has before starting to use Struggle, and if it has 1 hit KO moves like Self-Destruct
  3. Your best choice when setting up a team for hunting is having Pokemon with the moves False Swipe, Spore and defensive, non damaging moves like Substitute or Rest
  4. Having a lead with the ability Cute Charm makes it easier to find Pokemon of the opposite sex, very useful if your target only evolves if it's a certain gender, such as Salazzle, Gallade or Vespiqueen
  5. With most methods, you can safely put your console to sleep once you want to stop, but do not shut it down
  6. Use a Fast Ball at the start of every battle, since they have a higher capture chance if used during the first turn

Breeding

  1. Make sure your foreign Pokemon is a Ditto, since it can breed with any Pokemon, with the exception of legendaries and mythicals
  2. Having a Pokemon in your party with the ability Flame Body cuts hatching time in half for all eggs
  3. Some games have special powers that boost hatching time and the chance to get an egg. Combine these with the oval charm and Flame Body to make the entire process much faster.

Wormholes

  1. White wormholes will always have an Ultra Beast
  2. If the screen flashes once you enter a wormhole, you'll find a legendary
  3. The shiny chance caps at 5000 light years
  4. You can soft reset Ultra Beasts and legendaries, but not the normal Pokemon
  5. Having two of a legendary trio in your party will make it possible to find the third. For example:
    • Having Palkia and Dialga will unlock Giratina
    • Having Kyogre and Groudon will unlock Rayquaza

S.O.S

  1. To make sure you don't have to worry about Strugle, do the following set up:
    1. Get a Pokemon with the ability Harvest and the move Swill Swap
    2. Get a Pokemon with the move trick, or teach it to the first Pokemon, and give it a Leppa Berry to hold
    3. Once you enter the battle use Skill Swap to give the wild Pokemon Harvest and Trick to give it the Leppa Berry. Now, everytime its PP gets low it'll eat the berry and use Harvest to get it back
  2. The battle will end if both wild Pokemon are defeated, Don't use multi-hit attacks like Earthquake or Surf
  3. Using an Adrenaline Orb will make it more likely for the wild Pokemon to call for help

DexNav & Pokeradar

  1. Always use a repel, since encountering a different Pokemon from the chain is enough to break it
  2. With the Pokeradar, once you hit a max chain of 40, keep reseting the radar until the shiny patch appears
  3. There's always a small chance your chain will be broken each time you step on a patch of grass
  4. Leaving the are will break the chain

Chain fishing

  1. You don't have to catch or defeat the Pokemon, fishing it up is enough to keep up the chain
    • However, this means that failing to fish up anything will break the chain
  2. Just like with the DexNav/Pokeradar, leaving the are will break the chain
  3. Having a lead with the ability Suction Cups will make it easier to fish up Pokemon

Dynamax Adventures

  1. You won't know if a Pokemon is shiny until you get to the menu at the end of the raid
  2. You can only keep one of the caught Pokemon at the end of the raid

Random Encounters

  1. In Scarlet & Violet, making a sandwich with an Herba Mistica will give you Sparkling Power, which boosts the shiny chance of overworld Pokemon for a certain amount of time
    • This boost only applies to Pokemon that have the same typing as the power (Water Sparkling Power for water types, Dragon Sparkling Power for dragon types and so on)
  2. In Sword & Shield, repeatedly defeating Pokemon of the same species will increase it's Battle Count, the higher the number, the greater the chance of finding Brillant Pokemon, these Pokemon have higher odds of being shiny
    • the Battle Count is cumulative; you can increase this number over time

Soft Reseting

  1. This method is best used for static encounters, such as legendaries and mythicals
  2. The button combination for quickly restarting your DS/3DS is L + R + Start
    • You can tie rubber bands to your L and R buttons so you only need to press Start to reset the console
