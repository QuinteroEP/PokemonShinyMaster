Shiny Pokemon Master

Meet the Charms

Say hello to your two new best friends

The Shiny Charm

The greatest tool available, as long as you have it, All shiny odds are improved! Often by double their usual number. For instance, the base chance goes from 1/4096 to 1/2048. However, its difficulty to aquire matches its usefulness, as it requires you to complete the National dex of the game. That means, having caught every Pokemon available for that game at least once.

The Oval Charm

Not nearly as useful as the shiny charm, but if you opt for the Masuda method, it'll come in handy. It increases the chance of an egg appearing on the daycare. It's aquisition varies depending on the game. For instance, on some games you get it from the professor after catching every Pokemon on the Regional dex, not the National, while in other games you can simply get it by defeating a special trainer after beating the main story.

The other Charms

Newer generations have introduced even more charms. In the most recent games, Scarlet & Violet, we now have 6 different charms. Like the Exp, Mark and Catching charm, which increase the amount of Exp gained, the odds of getting a mark, and the odds of getting a critical capture respectively.
